# Formula Sheet: Quantitative Economics, Demography, Psychometry, Official Statistics

## Quantitative Economics

- Trend by least squares: fit `Y=a+bt`.
- Time series components: trend, seasonal, cyclical, irregular.
- ARIMA route: stationarity -> identify `p,d,q` -> estimate -> diagnose -> forecast.
- Laspeyres: base quantity weighted price index.
- Paasche: current quantity weighted price index.
- Fisher: geometric mean of Laspeyres and Paasche.
- Econometric diagnostics: multicollinearity, autocorrelation, heteroscedasticity.
- Simultaneous equations: order condition, rank condition, 2SLS.

## Demography

- Crude birth/death rates: event count divided by mid-year population times multiplier.
- ASFR: age-specific fertility rate.
- TFR: sum of ASFR across reproductive ages times interval width.
- GRR: female births per woman ignoring mortality.
- NRR: GRR adjusted for female survival.
- Life table route: `l_x -> d_x -> q_x/p_x -> L_x -> T_x -> e_x`.

## Psychometry

- Z score: `(X-mean)/sd`.
- T score: `50 + 10Z`.
- Reliability: consistency.
- Validity: measures intended construct.
- Spearman-Brown: reliability after changing test length.

## Official Statistics

Prepare agency tables for:

- Population: Census, SRS, NFHS.
- Prices: MoSPI CPI, WPI.
- Industry: ASI, IIP.
- Trade: DGCI&S.
- Banking: RBI DBIE.
- Labour: PLFS, Labour Bureau.
